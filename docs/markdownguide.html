Markdown formatting guide

Hey! You're probably here to check out what requirements are set by us to format your recipes complete.

First our supported MarkdownTags (only the text also our algorithm uses to format your recipes):

# Heading 1 for recipe title
## Heading 2 for part titles like Description, Materials and Steps
#### *** Bold and italic for time needed to cook recipe
* for material and steps list

So a formatted recipe could look like this (and SHOULD look like this to meet our requirements):

Pizza

for 4 person(s)

Description

A famous Italian dish

Time: 15 min

Materials

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Eggs

Steps

  • Make the dough...
  • Put the stuff you want on your pizza on the pizza
  • Put the pizza in the oven

And so it should look like when you format it (with md symbols):

# Pizza
for 4 person(s)
## Description
A famous Italian dish
#### ***Time: 15 min***
## Materials
* Flour
* Yeast
* Water
* Eggs

## Steps
* Make the dough...
* Put the stuff you want on your pizza on the pizza
* Put the pizza in the oven
